Accessories

Tank Accessories & Pump Packages

Hughes Tank Company offers a complete range of pump packages and tank accessories to complete your fuel storage installation. All pump packages are plumbed on our yard and tested before shipment — your tank arrives ready to put into service.

Features & Specifications

  • 15–100 GPM pump packages
  • 12-volt DC, electric, and gas-powered pump options
  • Digital and mechanical level gauges
  • Emergency pressure/vacuum vents
  • Fill caps, adapters, and fittings
  • Overfill prevention valves
  • Grounding and bonding kits
  • Saddle skids and mounting hardware
  • All accessories plumbed and tested before shipment
