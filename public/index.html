Frequently Asked Questions
What is UL 142 and do I need it?▼
UL 142 is the Underwriters Laboratories standard for aboveground steel tanks storing flammable and combustible liquids. NFPA 30 and the International Fire Code require tanks to be designed and constructed per an approved engineering standard — UL 142 is the most widely accepted. If your tank will store fuel above ground, you almost certainly need a UL 142-listed tank.
What is the difference between UL 2085 and Flameshield®?▼
Both are fire-protected aboveground tank systems with a two-hour fire rating, but they are listed under different standards. UL 2085 (Fireguard®) is listed by Underwriters Laboratories. Flameshield® is listed by STI (Steel Tank Institute). Which is required depends on your local Authority Having Jurisdiction.
How long does it take to get a tank?▼
We respond to every quote request within 24 hours, guaranteed. Lead times vary by tank size and configuration, but we consistently offer some of the shortest lead times in the industry. Contact us for current production availability on your specific configuration.
Do you build custom sizes?▼
Yes. Custom sizes are a core part of what we do. We can build tanks to virtually any dimension within the applicable UL standard's design parameters. Submit a quote request with your required dimensions and we will confirm feasibility and pricing.
Do tanks ship with inspector documentation?▼
Yes. Every UL-listed tank ships with the appropriate UL label and supporting documentation. We can also provide test records and manufacturer's declarations as required by your local inspector or permitting authority.
What pump packages are available?▼
We offer fully customizable pumping and metering packages from 15 to 100 GPM. Options include 12-volt DC, electric, and gas-powered pumps with a variety of meter and nozzle configurations. All packages are plumbed on our yard and tested before shipment.
What warranty is included?▼
All tanks come with a one-year manufacturer's warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Contact our sales team for specific warranty terms applicable to your configuration.
What areas do you serve?▼
We ship throughout the continental United States. We work with regional rep firms — including Wilson-Rogers & Associates (West), Walsh-Long (Midwest), and Mike Long (Southeast) — to provide local support and help you navigate local code requirements.
