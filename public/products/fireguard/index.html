Fire-Rated Aboveground

UL 2085 Fireguard® Fire-Rated Tanks

UL 2085

The UL 2085 Fireguard® tank provides two-hour fire resistance for aboveground fuel storage where local codes, fire marshals, or site conditions require fire protection. It also meets EPA secondary containment requirements for petroleum storage.

The Fireguard system uses a monolithic insulating concrete-like material between the primary and outer steel shells — providing both fire protection and secondary containment in a single listed assembly.

Features & Specifications

  • UL 2085 — two-hour fire rating
  • Meets EPA 40 CFR 112 secondary containment requirements
  • Integral secondary containment — no separate pan required
  • Available in a wide range of capacities and configurations
  • Custom sizes and pump packages available
  • Full UL 2085 label and documentation included
  • Air-tested before every shipment
  • One-year manufacturer's warranty
