Underground Storage

Permatank® Fiberglass Underground Tanks

STI-P3

The Permatank® is an STI-listed fiberglass underground storage tank (UST) built to STI-P3 specifications. Fiberglass construction eliminates the corrosion concerns associated with steel USTs and provides compatibility with a wide range of fuel products including ethanol and biodiesel blends.

All Permatank® tanks are manufactured to meet EPA underground storage tank regulations and ship with full STI documentation for your inspector and permitting authority.

Features & Specifications

  • STI-P3 listed fiberglass construction
  • Compatible with E0–E100 and B0–B100 fuel blends
  • Corrosion-resistant — no cathodic protection required
  • Meets EPA underground storage tank regulations
  • Available in standard and custom capacities
  • Full STI label and documentation included
  • One-year manufacturer's warranty
