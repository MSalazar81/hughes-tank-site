Fire-Protected Aboveground

Flameshield® Fire-Protected Tanks

STI Flameshield®

The Flameshield® system is a Steel Tank Institute (STI) listed fire-protected aboveground tank that uses intumescent material between the primary and secondary steel walls to provide a two-hour fire rating.

Flameshield® is a widely accepted alternative to UL 2085 Fireguard. Both meet the same performance requirements — check with your local Authority Having Jurisdiction (AHJ) to confirm which listing is required or accepted in your jurisdiction.

Features & Specifications

  • STI-listed — two-hour fire protection rating
  • Intumescent insulation between primary and secondary walls
  • Integral secondary containment
  • Accepted by most fire marshals and AHJs nationwide
  • Custom sizes and configurations available
  • Full STI label and documentation included
  • Air-tested before every shipment
  • One-year manufacturer's warranty
