Our Story

Built From the Ground Up

Hughes Tank Company was founded in 1989 in Venus, Texas. We started hand-welding small tanks for local farms and businesses — and a dedication to hard work and quality allowed the company to grow steadily over the following decades.

Today, Hughes Tank operates a 140,000+ square foot state-of-the-art manufacturing facility on 40 acres in Venus, TX, producing both aboveground and underground storage tanks for customers throughout the United States.

In 2023, Hughes Tank was acquired by MAG Capital Partners, which has invested significantly in facility expansion and new manufacturing capabilities — including a 48,000 sq ft addition completed in December 2024. The company continues to operate under the same quality standards and team that built its reputation.

We manufacture UL 142, UL 2085 Fireguard®, Flameshield®, Permatank®, and ACT-100-U fuel storage tanks. Every tank is air-tested on our yard before shipment. Pump and plumbing packages are available for an additional cost.
